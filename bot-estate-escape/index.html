🔍 The Clue

A wealthy person died without a will. Their estate is worth $500,000. According to intestacy laws, the surviving spouse gets the first $150,000 plus half of the remainder.

How much does the surviving spouse inherit?
Hint

Calculate: $150,000 + (remaining $350,000 ÷ 2)

📚 Why This Matters

Dying without a will (intestate) means state laws decide who gets your assets, not you. This can lead to outcomes you wouldn't have chosen.
