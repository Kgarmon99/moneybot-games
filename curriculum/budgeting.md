# MoneyBot Curriculum: Budgeting 101

## The 50/30/20 Rule
The ultimate cheat code for managing your cash flow:
- **50% Needs:** Rent, groceries, utilities, minimum debt payments. (The survival baseline).
- **30% Wants:** Dining out, gaming, subscriptions, fun. (The sanity baseline).
- **20% Savings/Investing:** Emergency fund, index funds, retirement. (The wealth-building baseline).

## The Emergency Fund Shield
Before you invest a single dollar, you need a shield. 
- **Level 1:** $1,000 in a high-yield savings account (HYSA). This stops 90% of minor emergencies from turning into credit card debt.
- **Level 2:** 3 to 6 months of living expenses. This is your "F-You" money that lets you walk away from toxic jobs or survive unexpected layoffs.

## The Enemy: Predatory Debt
Payday loans, high-interest credit cards, and "Buy Now, Pay Later" schemes are designed to trap you in a cycle of poverty. 
- If you have high-interest debt (over 10%), attack it relentlessly. It's a guaranteed negative return on your net worth. 
- Always pay your credit card statement balance IN FULL every month. Zero exceptions.
