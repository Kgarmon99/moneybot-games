# MoneyBot Brand & Visuals Bible

## Core Brand Identity
- **Brand Name:** MoneyBot
- **Mission:** Financial education through highly engaging, gamer-centric simulators and media.
- **Aesthetic:** Premium, cyber-hacker, Robinhood-style financial terminal, glassmorphism, neon glows on deep dark backgrounds.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Green (Money Green):** #00E676 (Neon, glowing, positive actions, wealth)
- **Primary Dark Green:** #00C853
- **Background Dark:** #0a0a1a (Deep space/cyber black)
- **Renter/Warning Red:** #FF3D00 (Predatory loans, debt, renting)
- **Buyer/Asset Blue:** #00B0FF (Homeownership, solid assets)

## The Mascot: MoneyBot
- **Description:** A sleek, premium white robot with glowing green eyes and cybernetic accents. 
- **Personality:** Confident, highly intelligent, encouraging coach.
- **Visuals:** Glossy 3D render, highly detailed, soft dramatic lighting. Often seen holding a glowing green shield to block bad financial decisions.

## 3D Models & Iconography (Visual References)
- **Banks:** Classic bank building with columns, glowing green accents.
- **Predatory Lenders:** Stack of cash with a red warning symbol.
- **Characters:**
  - *Gig Worker:* Modern ride-share car with a smartphone mounted on the dash.
  - *College Student:* Premium backpack with a graduation cap.
  - *Single Parent:* Cozy house with a family silhouette.
  - *Recent Grad:* Professional leather briefcase with an upward trend arrow.

## Instructions for Video/Image Generation
When generating content, B-roll, or visual overviews for MoneyBot:
1. MUST use the exact color palette (Black backgrounds, neon green text/highlights).
2. MUST feature the MoneyBot mascot as the guide.
3. NEVER use generic corporate stock photos (e.g., people shaking hands in suits).
4. ALWAYS use 3D, glossy, premium Robinhood-style UI aesthetics.
